This is a private client-access housing portal providing verified apartment availability in Upper Marlboro and Kettering, Maryland. All listings are curated and inspection-ready.

Inspection & Payment Policy

A $35 inspection access fee is required before any apartment inspection is scheduled.

If no apartment is suitable after inspection, the fee is refundable. All payments and agreements are handled directly between the client and Capital Crest Housing.
